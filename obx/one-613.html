Description

Description

New for 2019: This home is now Pet-Friendly!

This property could definitely be the 'one' for you this year! You will love this premiere location with fantastic ocean, sound, and lighthouse views. Two levels of furnished decks will ensure you get to see some gorgeous scenery and some beautiful sunrises and sunsets! Bordering National Park Service land and with boardwalk access to the beach, this property definitely delivers on a picturesque setting!

Inside, you can see the owner's TLC from the decorating to the kitchen with refrigerator and dining extras. The round granite table seats 10 with the bar seating 5, guaranteeing there will be plenty of space to enjoy those delicious vacation meals! The gas fireplace will provide a relaxing atmosphere in the living room with leather sofas to relax on, while you catch a show on the 42" plasma TV. The two-level tower with window seats is the perfect place to curl up and catch some more of those amazing views. After a fun day at the beach, 5 comfortable bedrooms will offer the perfect place to rest. Two master bedrooms are waiting, with 1 on the upper level and available sleeping options are 2 king beds, 2 queen beds and a pyramid bed with trundle.

Plenty of additional flat screen TVs, DVD players and a stereo/CD player will provide lots of entertainment options and a book library is also available for your enjoyment. At the end of the day, head outdoors to take a relaxing dip in the hot tub. Or use the available high-speed, wireless internet available for your laptop when you want to stay connected while on vacation. With so many great amenities and outstanding furnishings and decor, this home is not ONE to miss!

You will truly appreciate the views from this unique property ranging from the Pamlico Sound to the Atlantic Ocean. See our Avon webcam for a 180-degree view of Highway 12 between Avon and Buxton from the top of One. Check out beach access Ramp 38 and watch to see the wildlife roaming in the dunes while catching a private view of the Cape Hatteras Lighthouse day or night. Sit back and watch the camera as it pans from ocean to sound while dreaming of your next Hatteras Island beach vacation.

2019: Private Pool Available April 14th to October 20th.

Beds: 1 king, 1 king, 1 queen, 1 queen, 1 pyramid with trundle

Village

Village - Avon

Avon is the central village of Hatteras Island located about 30 miles south of Oregon Inlet and about 20 miles north of Hatteras Inlet. Here you will find Food Lion, the Island's only chain grocery store, gift shops, and local restaurants. The Avon Pier is a hot spot for fishing especially with the large Red Drum that flock here often.
