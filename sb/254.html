Description

Seas the Moment offers GORGEOUS serene coastal decor with a backdrop of the sparkling ocean and pristine beach... a true "home away from home" for the discriminating beach lover looking for something a "step above the rest". You will love the ENLARGED KITCHEN ISLAND and NEW 5 burner smooth cook-top, with gleaming granite counters. The free standing ice maker and dedicated wet bar, upholstered window seat, side by side refrigerator, generously stocked kitchen make for easy meal preparation and gracious entertaining. The new Pantry is perfect for extra food and beverage storage, too. The gently flowing plan of the main floor (2nd level) allows for easy living with kitchen, dining and living areas open to one another, as well as to the adjacent TV viewing room and spacious outside deck. Also on this floor are a large welcoming foyer with desk for your lap top and two bedrooms, each with their own private one-of-a-kind decorator bath with large, two person, tile shower. The ocean front bedroom on this level comes equipped with a queen bed, while the other bedroom, with limited ocean views, has two extra-long twin beds, perfect for the younger members in your family or those who prefer separate sleeping arrangements.
The top floor (3rd level) Master Bedroom Suite offers more of the good life, with its own view of the ocean from both the romantic four poster king sized bed as well as from the private deck. Its spacious bath complete with private water closet, whirlpool tub, double vanities, two person walk-in shower with double sets of controls operating the rain shower heads and multiple body jets, promises the ultimate bathing experience found in the best spas.
The two person elevator connecting the lower level with the main living area makes for an effortless experience on moving day and provides easy access to the 4th bedroom (queen) with its own private bath complete with tiled shower and whimsical ocean views. The laundry room and two additional outside accesses are also located on this floor.
Rounding out this vacation experience is the fenced in sand yard, outside enclosed shower, custom in-deck pool with ample lounging and dining furniture complete with deck chair and chaise cushions, market umbrellas and propane grille. In addition, all beds are made with high end linens, ready upon your arrival....making Seas the Moment the perfect spot for comfortably enjoying family and friends while watching the magnificent sunrises, soaring pelicans and the many pods of Dolphin for which Sandbridge Beach is famous.
CAC, CATV including 52 inch large flat screen TV in media room, DVD, 32 inch or larger TV's in every bedroom, wireless internet access, elevator and outside pool and shower....all just steps to the beach. Home provides Beach towels and beach chairs 1 per guest. Body wash and hand liquid soap , cleaning supplies, laundry supplies and spices are also provided. Fireplaces and electric storm shutters are not for guest use.
Bed Sizes: Br1- King, Br2 - Queen, Br3 - Queen, Br4 - Two Twins (Linen package and bed making service are provided on check-in day for weekly reservations year round.)
