Description

You will jump "Over the Moon" when you vacation in this beautiful oceanfront home. Built by the highly successful RBC Construction Team you are assured a rental that provides luxurious accommodations, a family-friendly layout, and amenities for everyone to enjoy.
This spectacular property offers three levels of living space, and 9 bedrooms...8 of which have private baths. This alone makes this a great property for several families to share! The elevator is a great addition to help move around this large home, as well as assist those in need. Bringing all of your luggage and groceries in upon arrival is just as easy as it can be!
The spacious first level is home to 5 bedrooms, each with King Suites and private bath. It's also an ocean-facing bedroom with access to the deck, for enjoying the beautiful ocean view and breezes. For added convenience, there is a gathering area in which to relax as well as a wet bar for additional beverage storage and grabbing a cool drink. There is access to the pool, hot tub and pool cabana too. You can enjoy a game of pool in the game room, which is great for cooling off after spending time outside on the decks and the beach.
The second floor is an open layout- with expansive views of the ocean across the entire east wall. The dining area can accommodate all 30 guests, and the kitchen is well prepared to handle cooking for this size of a group with 2 ovens, 2 refrigerators, a microwave, and cabinets well-stocked with all the utensils, cookware and gadgets you need to make a memorable meal. There is a Keurig Coffee Maker in the kitchen so bring your K Cups! The beautiful flooring matched with the stainless steel appliances, gorgeous granite counters, and plush decor in the living area is a fantastic frame for your vacation memories. Your family and friends will relax in the comfortable seating in the open living area, watching a game or movie on the large flat screen TV, feeling the warmth of the electric fireplace, and easy access to the huge ocean deck that spans the entire back of the home. Hours of sunshine and shade are yours to enjoy while watching the dolphins and pelicans play. The three bedrooms located on the 2nd level are Suites, with private baths for convenience.
The third floor finishes this incredible property with a theater room as well as a King bedroom, and a bath accessible to both. Ending your day in the privacy of your very own movie theater will sure to be a highlight of your stay at Over The Moon! For handicap friendly features, please contact our office for specific details of this home.
CAC, CCTVS including flat screens throughout. Internet Access. Pool, Hot Tub, Pool Cabana. Theater Room. Game Room with Pool Table. Outdoor Foosball Table. Elevator. Electric Fireplace available for off-season use only October-May. Gas Grill.
