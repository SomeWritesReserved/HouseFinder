Description

Summer Wind offers the ultimate oceanfront family vacation! Enjoy MAGNIFICENT OCEAN VIEWS and PRIVATE ACCESS TO THE BEACH directly from the pool deck. View the sunrise every morning from one of THREE OCEANFRONT MASTER BEDROOMS equipped with King-sized beds and luxurious private bathrooms (two with separate jetted tubs). Kids will have a blast sleeping in their own room with two bunk beds (two doubles/two twins). A second floor open concept living area complete with a new 70" 4K smart TV, two large dining tables, a spacious, well-equipped gourmet kitchen, and sweeping views of the ocean provides a place for the entire family to gather. With two refrigerators, two dishwashers, a separate ice maker, and extensive granite countertops, this kitchen was designed with large groups in mind. Head out onto the deck though one of two double sliding glass doors to enjoy the sight and sound of the waves while relaxing on nicely upholstered wicker lounge furniture, eating at the partially enclosed outdoor dining table, or cooking dinner on the Weber king size 6-burner gas grill. With a direct line to a large propane tank, you'll never have to worry about changing the tank or running out of fuel. Downstairs, a festive game room with wet bar (featuring a third refrigerator and dishwasher) and a 65" 4K smart TV allows for additional indoor fun including billiards, foosball, and a card/poker table. Step outside, and rolling sand dunes provide privacy while lounging by the Jacuzzi hot tub and in-ground oceanside salt water pool, complete with a glass fence for the ultimate beachfront experience. Kids of all ages will enjoy outdoor games including bocce, cornhole, and ladder golf. Plus, the fenced-in backyard and grassy front yard are perfect for your canine companion! Beautiful, Tuscany-inspired designer decor and artistic touches throughout the house will leave a lingering memory like the Summer Wind. Additional amenities include: laundry room with two dryers; flat screen TVs in every bedroom; multi-zone central air conditioning; wireless whole home high-speed internet; bright, LED lighting; assorted books, board games, Blu-Ray discs, and pool toys; and a gas fireplace (for off-season use October-May). There is also a deluxe Keurig Coffee Maker with iced coffee option (in addition to two regular coffee makers), so bring your K Cups. Weekly rentals during the peak season are Saturday to Saturday only.

New in 2018: Weber 6-burner gas grill; 7-person outdoor Jacuzzi hot tub; two large 4K smart TVs (for common areas); all new exterior window/door screens; cushions for all outdoor furniture; kitchen small appliances (coffee makers, toasters), professional knives, and high-end flatware; whole-home LED lighting upgrades; games, books, and Blu-Ray DVDs

New in 2019: Pool salt water conversion and new liner; Upgraded dishes, glasses and serving/baking ware
