Description

"You will fall in love with this spectacular semi-ocean front home loaded with amenities! Gorgeous furnishings and appointments throughout, with a third level gourmet kitchen and great room. Multiple decks with gorgeous ocean views! The spacious floor plan easily accommodates multiple families with ample living space and seven master suites. Master bathroom #1 boasts a large shower with body sprays and a separate Jacuzzi tub. Equipped with a multi-room/multi-source audio system with XM Radio, IPOD Dock, and flat panel TVs with DVD in every room. Spacious game room with a wet bar and pool table. CAC, ceiling fans throughout, high-speed wireless internet, basketball, water volleyball, gas grill. FIREPLACE NOT FOR GUEST USE. Large inground swimming pool with cabana. Easy beach access. NO SMOKING.

Bed Sizes: Br1- King, Br2 - King, Br3 - King, Br4 - King, Br5 - Queen, Br6 - Queen, Br7 - 2 Bunksets (T/T), Br8 - 2 Bunksets (T/T)"
