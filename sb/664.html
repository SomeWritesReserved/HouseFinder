Description

Unbelievable views of the bay and a center view of sunsets from all three levels of living space will awe and relax you during your Sandbridge beach vacation!
With new ownership and new management, Anchor Bay is now available as a great vacation choice for an active family in all seasons! The owner has made some terrific upgrades, including new countertops, many new decorations, furniture, flooring, blankets, pillows, and mattresses with a goal of making guests' family time as enjoyable as possible.
Anchor Bay is in a quiet cul-de-sac location with a large completely fenced grassed yard, which gives extra security from the bay and traffic for families with smaller children.
The top floor of Anchor Bay offers an ASTOUNDING VIEW of the bay from the large great room with vaulted ceilings, large windows, screened porch, and access to a wrap-around deck for enjoying the view and summer breezes. Your nightly entertainment will be relaxing with the entire family on the large leather sectional sofa (new for 2018) while watching the spectacular sunsets over the water. The large great room is open to the kitchen area, with counter top seating for 4, and to the dining area, with a large "farm table" big enough to seat everyone, and a convenient half-bath.
The large flat screen Ultra HD smart TV with DVD player and a good selection of movies will provide additional relaxing entertainment. From the kitchen area there is a cozy "den", with a comfortable place to watch another flat screen TV (with DVD player), read a great book, complete one of the provided puzzles, play a game, or sit quietly reflecting on the day.
The second floor of Anchor Bay offers TWO King Master Suites with access to the middle level deck that is shady and cool. The "Captain's Quarters" Master Suite is huge with large windows to capture the expansive views of the bay. Watch the sun sparkle on the waters of the bay as you are enjoying your coffee in bed in the morning, and watch the sunset as you relax for the night. A comfy reclining chair beckons you to some "alone time" in your private oasis to read a book or to watch your own show on the flat screen Smart TV. It offers a large master bathroom, with separate tub/shower and a new double-vanity sink. The second King Master Suite is also a roomy and private getaway, with its own full bath, comfy reclining chair, and flat screen Smart TV for your entertainment.
The first floor of Anchor Bay offers a Queen Master Suite with a full bath, new furnishings, and great views of the bay. There is a book shelf offering a small library of books (including children's books) for guests to read, and a flat screen smart TV for your entertainment. A second large bedroom on the first floor has a Queen bed and two Twin/Twin bunk beds, and yet another large flat screen TV with DVD player! There is a full bath in the hallway, as well as an air hockey table for inside fun. Laundry is also on the first floor.
The ocean sand and sunrises are a nice stroll away along a wide side street.
CAC, CCTVs including flat screen TV's in all bedrooms and den, and large Ultra HD flat screen in the great room, wireless internet access, DVD, screened porch, dock, boat ramp (***small non-motorized boats like kayaks or canoes or small motorized boats ONLY), gas grill, charcoal grill, air hockey, basketball hoop, fenced yard. *** Available games include a basketball and hoop at the driveway, soccer ball, corn hole, and two small bicycles for children to enjoy.
Bed Sizes: Br1 - King, Br2 - King, Br3 - Queen, Br4 - Queen and 2 Bunksets (Twin/Twin)
PET FRIENDLY HOUSE...25 POUND LIMIT.
