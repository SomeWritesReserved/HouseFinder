Description

Like a beautiful enchanting song of the sea, the new luxury oceanfront villa "Aria" will create lasting memories of treasured time spent at the coast. Upscale amenities are fine tuned for the most deserving of travelers: Eleven bedrooms with private baths...Seven are King Master Suites; Doubled-Up Gourmet Kitchen (two gas ranges, 2 dishwashers, 2 refrigerators, walk-in pantry); Outdoor Pool, Cabana and Gazebo; Indoor Heated pool; Hot Tub; Screened Porch; Game Room; Theater Room; Fitness Room; Arcade; Elevator. Three living levels with large gathering areas, expertly decorated. A unique location, being the first residential home on the south side of Sandbridge Beach, allowing a more expansive view of the ocean to the north. Aria will leave you looking for nothing...except more time!
This year-round-use home boasts a heated indoor swimming pool serviced by a separate convenient half bath. Large glass windows bring the blue sea inside. The outdoor swimming pool is surrounded by spacious decks, comfortable lounging furniture, tables and chairs for outside dining. The hours of enjoyment are extended from the useful pool cabana, which offers a protected food prep area and beverage storage with a wet bar and counter tops. The second level gazebo has amazing views of the ocean and provides a peaceful area for reading a book and taking a siesta.
The lower ground floor level is home to the Game Room, with a foosball table, poker table, large air hockey table and a pool table. The morning kitchen is a very nice treat in this large home, as it offers a small refrigerator, counter and cabinet space with a sink, perfect for coffee and beverages throughout the day. The fitness room is nicely equipped with workout machines and a TV so you can continue your fitness routine while on vacation. The Arcade Room offers games of all types, making time spent indoors much more fun. One of two laundry rooms is located on the 1st (the second is on the 3rd), and an elevator services all three levels of Aria, allowing for easy mobility for everyone for any need. Three bedroom suites each have their own private full bath, and an additional half bath has been added near the gym for convenience.
The middle second level, with its two story glass alcove, has floor-to ceiling views, creating the perfect backdrop for the spacious dining area with multiple tables to accommodate all 38 guests, as well as an incredible kitchen for the most advanced chef. Food prep and meal planning will be things you will want to do, as the kitchen is well stocked with everything you need to plan and prepare for your guests. Two of almost everything as well, with all stainless steel appliances - two gas ranges, two dishwashers, two refrigerators. Combine these with the gorgeous granite counters, custom cabinetry, and a walk in pantry to use for food and beverage storage. The adjoined screened in porch adds an additional location for outside protected dining. The great room has a built-in electric fireplace as well as a large flat-screen TV. The near-by Theater Room, with seating for 15, is an incredible place to enjoy family movie night or your favorite sporting event. Two bedrooms suites each have private bath, and an additional hall half bath is available, located just outside of the Theater Room. A large deck walks you directly onto the Gazebo and overlooks the outside pool area. Simply beautiful!
The upper third level has a central gathering area, also boasting a "morning kitchen" with small refrigerator, counter space and sink. The beautiful and comfortable furnishings enhance the upper open atrium view, which is an oceanic masterpiece fit for kings and queens. The final six bedrooms all offer private baths as well as ocean facing private decks. A third half hall bath as well as a second laundry room conveniently service this upper level.
Aria. 11 Bedrooms, 11 Bathrooms, Four Half Baths. Accommodates 38 guests.
CAC, CCTVS Throughout including all bedrooms and gathering areas. Theater, Game, Arcade, and Exercise Rooms. Outdoor Pool & Hot Tub. Indoor Heated Pool. Elevator. Screened Porch. Gas Grills. Fireplace available for off-season use only October-May.
Bed Sizes: Br1 - King, Br2 - King, Br3 - King, Br4 - King, Br5 - King, Br6 - King, Br7 - King, Br8 - 2 Queens, Br9 - 2 Queens, Br10 - 2 Bunk sets (Twin/Double) with a Twin Trundle each (sleeps 8), Br11 - 2 Bunk sets (Twin/Double) with a Twin Trundle each (sleeps 8)
Please Note: This vacation home books full weeks only, Saturday to Saturday, May 26 through October of each year. 2020 reservations booked in 2019 will require a 25% non-refundable payment within 24 hours of the booking date. The remainder of the first payment will be due in September 2019. "
