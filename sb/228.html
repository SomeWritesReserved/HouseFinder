Description

"Enjoy a wonderful view of the ocean from the cheery great room, bedrooms and front deck of this nice home located at the quiet north end of the beach. From the front deck or the large, wide sandy beach you can see ships coming and going to the Hampton Roads ports. The 2 car sheltered carport by the main entrance makes unloading and loading vehicles very convenient. The two decks facing east and west have non-splinter decking and offer a sunny or shady relaxing place. A well equipped kitchen will make meal preparation easy and simple. This comfortable home will make your vacation a memorable one. CAC, CCTV, VCR, DVD, stereo/cassette, ceiling fans, 2 refrigerators, vanity/dressing room by master bedrooms, gas grill, open decks with non-splinter decking, 2 swings, outside enclosed shower, internet access. NO PETS ALLOWED.

Bed Sizes: BR 1- King, BR 2 - Queen, BR 3 - 2 Twins, BR 4 - 2 Twins, BR 5 - Bunkset (Twin/Twin)"
