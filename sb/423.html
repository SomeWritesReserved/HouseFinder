Description

Majestic 10 Bedroom Luxury Home sits high on her throne overlooking the beautiful white sandy beach and sparkling blue Atlantic Ocean. Boasting a beautiful backyard oasis with an in-ground pool, hot tub, 2 story outdoor gazebo (ground level has granite counters and bar seating along with a flat screen TV, and the upper level offers see-the-horizon ocean views), and ample lounge chairs and patio furniture for your relaxation and enjoyment.
Bedrooms, Baths, and Gathering Areas on each of the three levels allows for privacy and plenty of room to spread out. First floor indoor heated pool can be used all year long, great for Holiday and Off-Season Rentals as well as use during the occasional summer storm. Your entire family can enjoy both the game room and gym, for kids of all ages have something to suit their interest and age! Game room has a billiards table for challenging games of pool. There is a "child-sized" game table which can switch to different game activities: pool table, ping pong, and air hockey. There is even a foosball table and a poker table! And the nearby gym, which over-looks the pool, has a Nordic Track Tread Mill, Elliptical Trainer, Universal Gym and Stationary Child Friendly Exercise Bike.
The gourmet kitchen on the second floor offers large dining tables for mealtime, as well as plenty of seating in front of the fireplace and large flat screen TV. The stainless steel high-end appliances include two JennAir gas ranges, two dishwashers, two refrigerators, toasters, and a nice selection of kitchen cook ware and gadgets. There is an amazing view of the ocean from the middle second floor living area and kitchen, with access onto the second floor deck and second level of the gazebo, a great place for watching the dolphins and pelicans. There is a theater room (seats 15) with an 80 inch (WOW) Blu-Ray HD TV with Harman Kardon Sound System***. A convenient wet bar will provide additional beverage storage and serving area.
The spacious third level gathering area provides a restful place to enjoy the view, read a book, or spend time on your computer. There is a wet bar with mini fridge as well for your convenience.
All bedrooms offer Flat Screen TVs, beautiful linens and decor, and most with private baths. (This home has 10 full baths and two half baths.) The lower level of the Gazebo offers a sink, refrigerator, and beautiful granite counters to dining poolside! Two gas grills and an outdoor hot and cold shower.
The first floor can accommodate an "assistance needed" family member or friend, with a separate door directly from the driveway into a King Bedroom. The bedroom has a private bath. The elevator will allow movement on all three levels.
Indoor Heated Pool, Outdoor Pool, Hot Tub, Elevator, High Speed Internet Access, Game Room (Pool Table, Foosball, Poker Table, childrens' 3 in 1 Game Table), Gym, Home Theater. Fireplace for off-season use only October-May. No smoking, no pets. For handicap friendly features, please contact our office for specific details of this home.

Bed Sizes: Br1 - King, Br2 - King, Br3 - King, Br4 - King, Br5 - King, Br6 - King, Br7 - King, Br8 - 2 Doubles, Br9 - 2 Bunksets (Twin/Double) with 2 Twin Trundles, Br10 - 2 Bunksets (Twin/Double) with 2 Twin Trundles

Oceanic Serenade accepts reservations for full weeks only (Saturday to Saturday) May 26th to October 31st of each year."

Danielle Haudricourt writes:

This house was smartly and beautifully designed for oceanfront living. 180 degree beach & ocean views from the main living/kitchen area, makes for a pleasant environment in which to prepare meals and enjoy eating them! With 6 of the 10 bedrooms on the top floor, all of them have a view of either the ocean or the bay, everyone felt they got a special room. The bunk rooms were fantastic, and included child/baby friendly items I've not seen in other rental homes. The theater room was a huge hit with our kids/teens, along with the game room downstairs. Parking was great, with 6-8 vehicles fitting on the driveway, in addition to street parking, we didn't have to shuffle cars around every day. This house really had everything!

Shannon Kosko - Hudson, OH writes:

The house was magnificent! Plenty of room for our family vacation of 30 people. The kitchen was so well stocked. The best I have ever seen in a rental home. The family room was spacious and comfy. A favorite spot in the house for the adults was the 2nd floor sitting area with the leather couches - away from the tv and a beautiful view of the ocean. The bedrooms were tastefully decorated and spacious. The 2nd floor laundry was a bonus. One of my favorite features of the home was around the outdoor pool. Instead of a solid fence going all around the pool, there was a clear, heavy plastic like portion of the fence to offer an unobstructed view of the ocean. The only complaint we had was the lack of room darkening blinds or curtains in the bedrooms. No one wants to wake up with the sun on vacation, so we had to come up with some makeshift curtains of our own.
